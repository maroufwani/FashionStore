Care Instructions:
Taking proper care of your Men's Cotton Shirt with Box Pattern will help maintain its quality and appearance. Here are some care instructions to follow:

Machine Wash: You can safely machine wash this shirt. Use cold or lukewarm water (not hot) to prevent any shrinkage or color fading.
Gentle Cycle: When using a washing machine, choose the gentle or delicate cycle to protect the fabric.
Mild Detergent: Use a mild, color-safe detergent to maintain the vibrancy of the shirt's colors and patterns.
Avoid Bleach: Do not use bleach or harsh chemicals, as they can damage the fabric and patterns.
Separate Colors: If washing with other garments, separate colors to prevent color bleeding.
Turn Inside Out: To protect the box pattern and buttons, turn the shirt inside out before washing.
Fasten Buttons: Fasten all the buttons on the shirt to prevent them from snagging on other items during washing.
Avoid Overloading: Do not overload the washing machine, as this can cause excessive wrinkling and reduce the effectiveness of cleaning.
Dry Carefully: After washing, reshape the shirt and hang it on a hanger to air dry. Avoid direct sunlight, as it can fade colors over time.
Ironing: If necessary, iron the shirt on a medium heat setting. Iron the inside of the shirt to avoid direct contact with the box pattern.
Dry Cleaning: While the shirt is suitable for machine washing, for the best care, consider professional dry cleaning when needed.

By following these care instructions, you can ensure that your Cotton Shirt maintains its quality, colors, and pattern, allowing you to enjoy its stylish and comfortable features for a long time.
